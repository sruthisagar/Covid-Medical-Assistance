PRECAUTIONS

Protect yourself and others around you by knowing the facts and taking appropriate precautions. Follow advice provided by your local health authority.

IF YOU'RE AT A PUBLIC PLACE

  • Clean your hands often
  • Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing
  • Wear a mask at all times
  • Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze
  • Carry a hand sanitizer with you
  • Avoid spaces that are closed, crowded or involve close contact

IF AT HOME

  • As much as possible, keep away from other people and pets in your home
  • Use separate dishes, glasses, cups, and eating utensils
  • If possible, stay in a bedroom and use a bathroom separate from other people in the home
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue, throw the tissue away
  • Wash with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, or use alcohol-based hand sanitizer

COMMON SYMPTOMS

DRY COUGH ??
  • Honey can be used to treat day and nighttime dry coughing
  • Try taking cough drops
  • Vapourisers and steamy showers may also help

OR FEVER ??
  • Drink plenty of liquids
  • Avoid as much physical activity as possible

SUFFERING FROM SORE THROAT ??
  • Drink plenty of liquids
  • Gargle with warm salt water
  • Try sucking on lozenges
  • Take honey mixed in tea or on its own

FEELING TOO TIRED ??
  • Have regular sleep schedule
  • Eat a balanced diet.
  • Get regular exercise
  • Avoid caffeine

HAVING DIARRHOEA ??
  • Replacing lost fluids with an oral rehydration
  • Antidiarrhoeal drugs such as loperamide

SUFFERING FROM LOSS OF SMELL ??
  • Lemon helps to restore back the sense of smell
  • Use saline nasal drops as recommended
  • Nasal steroid spray as recommended

SERIOUS SYMPTOMS

  • Difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement

If you have any of these symptoms, please test for covid in a nearby covid test center
